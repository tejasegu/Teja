About Our Company

NuWash is a mobile car care service based in Austin, Texas. Our mission is to make taking care of your car easier than ever. Currently, we provide convenient car washing services by sending a professional technician to come to you and wash your car right where its parked at your office, apartment, or home. Originally started by a small team of friends at The University of Texas in 2016, NuWash now serves thousands of happy customers across Austin and Dallas, Texas.
